Tempe Goreng

Savory Crispy Tempe Goreng, Best Served with Hot Rice and Sambal

Savory and crunchy dish that is often served as a snack or side dish. Tempe goreng is made by frying tempe, which is a fermented soybean product.

Tempe has a nutty and earthy flavor that pairs well with the spices used in tempe goreng. The spices typically used in tempe goreng include garlic, onion, turmeric, and chili powder

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tempeh
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbs of coriander powder
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of chicken stock powder
  • 300 ml of warm water
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Cut the block of tempe into 10 slices
  2. Grind the garlic, mix it with warm water, coriander powder, salt, and chicken stock powder. Stir until well combined
  3. Add the tempe into the mixture until it is submerged. Let it stand for about 10 minutes for the spices to soak in
  4. Fry the tempe in hot oil over medium heat until golden brown. Remove from the heat and drain
